Duoc Son Joint Stock Company

We was established in July 2009 with the main fields of production and sales of medicine and healthcare products for human, made from 100% natural materials and friendly with consumers.

With dynamic, creative, highly skilled and enthusiastic leadership, staff and employees. Currently, Duoc Son JSC has become one of the leading claims its position on the domestic and international pharmaceutical market.

Vietnam is a country famous for abundant and rich medicinal resources with all kinds of rare medicines. Duoc Son has formed and pursued the path of sales, production, farming, exploitation of such resources in order to export pharmaceutical raw materials, herds of Vietnam, and to produce pharmaceutical consumable products.

Our Vision

"Become a leading group in manufacturing and providing medicines and consumable drugs, tea products, pharmaceutical cosmetics and health care products, Oriental medicine from 100% natural, clean and safe herbs".

Build a system of supermarkets specializing in wholesale and retail of herbal products, high-end hospitals specializing in treatment combining with southeast medicine.

Create a sustainable position by exporting clean pharmaceutical raw materials, consumable pharmaceutical products in most of countries in the world.

Duoc Son always strives for continuous studying, improvement and creation to produce new products that are convenient and good for health. To make Duoc Son a strong and sustainable group, our core value is: “Community's health is a measure for success”

Mission

Offer consumers pharmaceutical products derived from herbs that are good for health and have competitive prices.

By applying advanced science and technology, the Company will help Vietnam's pharmaceutical industry produce valuable product lines and generate income for Vietnamese people, especially the residents in mountainous, remote and countryside areas, etc.
