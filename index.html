Duoc Son Joint Stock Company

We was established in July 2009 with the main fields of production and sales of medicine and healthcare products for human, made from 100% natural materials and friendly with consumers.

With dynamic, creative, highly skilled and enthusiastic leadership, staff and employees. Currently, Duoc Son JSC has become one of the leading claims its position on the domestic and international pharmaceutical market.

Vietnam is a country famous for abundant and rich medicinal resources with all kinds of rare medicines. Duoc Son has formed and pursued the path of sales, production, farming, exploitation of such resources in order to export pharmaceutical raw materials, herds of Vietnam, and to produce pharmaceutical consumable products.
